# Use Cases

## Lost MFA Device

A user knows the password but lost the phone with the authenticator app. The assistant identifies official recovery options and helps prepare proof.

## Changed Phone Number

A user cannot receive SMS codes after changing numbers. The assistant checks recovery email, trusted devices, backup codes, and service-specific procedures.

## Locked Social Account

A user is locked out after suspicious activity. The assistant helps avoid repeated failed attempts and prepares a calm support request.

## Small Business Admin Loss

A small business lost access to a domain, ads account, or cloud admin account. The assistant gathers billing, domain, company, and admin proof.

## Estate Recovery

A family member needs official access to a deceased person’s account. The assistant explains that estate procedures and legal documents may be required.

## Post-Recovery Hardening

After access is restored, the assistant helps prevent the same failure from happening again.

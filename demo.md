# Demo

## Scenario: Lost Authenticator App

The user lost a phone that had the authenticator app for a work email account.

## Step 1: Diagnosis

The assistant asks:

- Which service is affected?
- Do you still know the password?
- Do you have backup codes?
- Do you have access to recovery email or phone?
- Are you an individual user, employee, admin, or estate representative?

## Step 2: Recovery Plan

The assistant produces a safe checklist:

- Try backup codes first.
- Check whether trusted devices are still logged in.
- Use the official account recovery page.
- Prepare identity and ownership evidence.
- Contact workspace admin if this is a managed account.
- Avoid repeated random attempts that may increase lockout risk.

## Step 3: Support Message

The assistant generates a concise support message that states the facts, lists available proof, and avoids suspicious language.

## Step 4: After Recovery

The assistant recommends:

- regenerate backup codes;
- add a passkey;
- update recovery email and phone;
- store recovery data in a password manager;
- document business admin access.

## Demo Safety Rule

The demo must never show bypass, phishing, social engineering, password cracking, or unauthorized access.

# Privacy Note

Account Recovery Assistant is designed to help users prepare for official
account recovery flows without collecting passwords, backup codes, SMS codes,
passport scans, or other sensitive secrets.

## Local Use

In the current local CLI and local web wizard:

- recovery plans are generated locally;
- the tool does not require accounts or cloud sign-in;
- the project is intended to avoid collecting or transmitting recovery secrets.

## What Users Should Not Enter

Do not enter:

- passwords;
- backup codes;
- SMS codes;
- authenticator codes;
- full passport or national ID scans;
- payment card numbers.

The tool is for guidance, evidence preparation, and official-channel recovery
planning only.

## Hosted Version Warning

If a hosted version is introduced later, its data retention and privacy policy
must be documented separately before public rollout.

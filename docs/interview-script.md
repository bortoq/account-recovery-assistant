# User Interview Script

## Screening

- Have you ever lost access to an important account?
- Which service was involved?
- Was it personal, business, or admin/workspace access?
- Did MFA, phone number, recovery email, identity review, or suspicious activity play a role?

## Problem Discovery

- What did you try first?
- What was confusing or stressful?
- Which official help pages did you find?
- Did any action make the situation worse?
- What evidence did the provider ask for?

## MVP Reaction

Show the local wizard or a Markdown report.

Ask:

- Is the next best action clear?
- Is anything too generic?
- Are the warnings useful or annoying?
- Are the official links trustworthy?
- Would you use this before contacting support?

## Outcome And Willingness To Pay

- Would this have saved time or reduced mistakes?
- Would you prefer self-serve or human-assisted guidance?
- Would you pay for a detailed plan? How much?
- Would a business pay for recovery readiness planning?

## Closing

- What is missing before you would trust this?
- Can we follow up after adding your scenario?
- Do not share passwords, codes, IDs, or private account data.

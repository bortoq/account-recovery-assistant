# Landing Page Drafts

These are copy drafts for validation pages, not final legal or SEO content.

## Lost Google Authenticator

**Headline:** Lost your Google authenticator app?

**Promise:** Get a safe checklist for using Google's official recovery flow, preparing ownership evidence, and avoiding repeated attempts that can make review harder.

**CTA:** Generate a local recovery plan.

## Apple Trusted Device Lost

**Headline:** Lost access to your trusted Apple device or phone?

**Promise:** Understand whether to use a trusted device, trusted phone, or Apple account recovery wait period before you reset anything.

**CTA:** Prepare your Apple recovery plan.

## Instagram Or Facebook Hacked

**Headline:** Facebook or Instagram account hacked?

**Promise:** Follow official hacked-account paths, collect identity or business proof, and avoid duplicate reports or unsafe recovery services.

**CTA:** Build a safe takeover response checklist.

## Microsoft Account Or Admin Lockout

**Headline:** Locked out of Microsoft account or Microsoft 365 admin access?

**Promise:** Separate personal Microsoft account recovery from Microsoft 365 tenant/admin support and prepare the right proof before escalating.

**CTA:** Choose consumer or tenant recovery path.

## Trust Boundary For All Pages

- Official channels only.
- No bypasses or hacking.
- No guaranteed recovery.
- Do not enter passwords, backup codes, SMS codes, or identity document numbers.
- Local alpha; hosted data handling must be documented separately.
